Apartment Reviews?

If you lived on campus freshman year then you can apply for 2YLC which gives you the option to live in lauterber. It’s the best building for sophomores and you’ll have priority to live in Tubman/Chavez the following year. If you have U3 standing then try to get into Tubman/chavez or West Apartments. Otherwise try to get into Roth then Kelly and Tabler as your last resort. If you want a single then you’ll have to live in corridor style which I wouldn’t recommend

Best way to look for off-campus apartments?

Grad. students and postdocs often live off campus either in accessory apartments, which are connected to houses or in shared rented houses. Someone mentioned the Facebook group, which is a good place to look. I would also reach out to your department and your department coordinator / staff to see if they know of anybody that is looking for someone either take over a lease or are looking to fill a spot. Just be aware that there are some sketchy accessory apartments so I would recommend looking up whether the house/appartment has a rental permit through the Brookhaven town website.
