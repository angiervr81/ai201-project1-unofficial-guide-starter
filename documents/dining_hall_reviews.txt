Both dining halls are OK, though east on its good days is really good and better than west. On their bad days, east is arguably much worse.

For the omelettes go before the 8:00 classes finish in the morning, or really before 9. 7AM for those that get up ungodly early is empty and very serene.

Hospital Cafeteria adds variety and is always open (Winter and Summer even) and it uses Dining Dollars

On Breaks and during Finals dining hall hours are shortened *Dining Hall is the best whenever parents are coming to campus, it tends to be the least tasty days prior

The salad bar will occasionally have less desirable fruits or veggies since they leave it out all day or it’s freezer-burnt. If it tastes bad or it doesn’t taste how it normally does DON’T EAT IT
