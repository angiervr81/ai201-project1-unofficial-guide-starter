How do I request a dorm bed to be lofted?

Log into fixit, create a new work order, put in your building and room number (your specific bedroom, not just the suite), and select furniture for the problem description drop down menu.

In the details box, tell them which side of the room you're on (wait until you actually move in so that you know this), and be specific about what you want them to do; ie. do you just want the bed raised or actually lofted like so that you can put your desk underneath? If just raised, how high do you want it raised to?

From what I understand, requests to loft beds typically take longer to fulfill and aren't guaranteed to be granted because they don't have enough hardware to loft every bed on campus.
